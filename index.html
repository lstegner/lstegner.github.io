I create robotic systems to help people.

My research is at the intersection of Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Robotics, and Healthcare. Motivated by global challenges posed by increasing caregiver shortages, I seek to design, build, and evaluate robotic systems that support complex tasks in healthcare environments. A core aspect of my work lies in engaging with community partners to bring robots into the real world. Previously, I received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Cincinnati in 2019 and my M.S./Ph.D. in Computer Science from UW-Madison in 2022 and 2025, respectively.
